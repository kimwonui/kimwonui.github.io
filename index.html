About

01 / Profile

Born in South Korea, I attended Storm King School in the U.S. during high school and went on to Penn State for my Economics degree. Marketer and content builder who likes the part most people skip — finishing.

I study Economics at Penn State, lead IC (a ~30-person creative studio making video, design, and writing for Gen Z), and run overseas market research for SpoAny, a major Korean fitness brand. I’ve shipped my own iOS app, KeyGlo.

Before all that I served 18 months in the ROK Marine Corps as a Sergeant, where I learned to own the details and finish what I start.
